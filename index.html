About Me

I am a Furniture Designer and Manufacturing Engineer specializing in the development of modular furniture systems that integrate creative design with real-world production feasibility.

My expertise lies in transforming concepts into scalable products by combining structural logic, cost intelligence, vendor feasibility analysis, and production workflow planning. I work across retail environments, residential furniture, workspace systems, and engineered product development — ensuring every design is optimized not only for aesthetics, but also for manufacturing efficiency and long-term durability.

With a strong foundation in engineering-led design, I focus on creating furniture solutions that are modular, installation-friendly, and production-ready from the very beginning of the development cycle.
